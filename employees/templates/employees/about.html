About Employee Management System

This is a simple employee management system built with Django and Bootstrap 5. It allows you to manage employees and departments in your organization.

Features include:

  • Add, edit, and delete employees
  • View employee list with pagination
  • Search functionality
  • Department management
Built with Django 4.2 and Bootstrap 5
